# 📊 HR Attrition Dashboard | ABC Company

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive HR Attrition Dashboard designed for a hypothetical company (ABC) to monitor employee turnover, identify high-risk segments, and support data-driven HR decisions.

With 6+ years of corporate experience, this project focuses on solving real-world business problems through actionable insights and intuitive dashboard design.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Track employee attrition trends  
- Identify key drivers of employee turnover  
- Enable HR teams to monitor workforce metrics  
- Support data-driven retention strategies  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Tableau (Dashboard & Visualization)  
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Sales department shows the highest attrition levels, indicating potential workload or performance pressure  
- Employees under 25 contribute significantly to attrition, highlighting early-career retention challenges  
- Frequent business travel is associated with higher employee turnover  
- Lower job satisfaction scores correlate strongly with increased attrition  

---

## 💡 Business Impact

This dashboard enables organizations to:

- Identify high-risk employee segments and take proactive action  
- Improve retention strategies through targeted interventions  
- Optimize workforce planning and HR policies  
- Monitor attrition trends in real-time  

---

## 🚀 Use Case

This solution can be used by HR teams and business leaders to:

- Track attrition KPIs regularly  
- Analyze workforce trends across departments and demographics  
- Make informed decisions to improve employee engagement and retention
